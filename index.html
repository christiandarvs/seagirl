SEAGIRL

a wanderer by the sea,

a creature rare to see,

her song drifts over me,

filling me with ecstasy.


her beauty is alluring,

the waves around her roaring,

yet her hums are calming,

it feels like I'm worshipping.


I fell, longing for your sweet kiss,

why should I resist?

drag me to the abyss,

an offering to you, my seagirl.
